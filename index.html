Seoul

Seoul is the capital of South Korea home to sleek skyscrapers and shopping malls, as well as well-preserved royal palaces and Buddhist temples.

A 24-hour city, Seoul has an intense work-hard, play-hard culture. The booming metropolis fuses tradition and modernity. Seoul citizens have a deep respect for higher ranking individuals and pride in their venerable city.
